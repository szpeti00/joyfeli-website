About Me

Hi, I'm Feli Aarsman. I moved to Wageningen for university, fell in love with the city, and decided to stay. Over time, my focus shifted from academics to a passion for sports and staying active. From outdoor adventures like climbing and kayaking to indoor activities like spinning and Pilates.

Nature and human connection have always been at the heart of what I do. In summer, I work as a white-water kayaking instructor in Slovenia, and in winter, I coach in the gym and lead group fitness classes. With over 8 years of teaching experience, I'm guided by a belief in the power of positive energy to foster strength, happiness, and a brighter world for all.
